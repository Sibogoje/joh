What We Do

Community Engagement

Grassroots mobilization through 42 community circles across four regions, creating safe spaces for women empowerment.

Rights Advocacy

Community-driven advocacy promoting equality, human rights, and addressing systemic injustices through grassroots campaigns.

Economic Empowerment

Supporting women to start income-generating programs, addressing poverty as a key driver of gender-based violence.

Leadership Training

Transformative feminist leadership training educating women on their rights, governance, and feminist principles.
