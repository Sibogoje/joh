About Journey of Hope

A feminist-driven organization focused on addressing violations of the rights of women and girls, championing the One Billion Rising Campaign in Eswatini.

Our Mission

To empower and protect young girls and women in their communities so that they can live in safe environments.

Our Vision

A society where young girls and women live happily in safe, caring and stimulating environment that promotes the best of their potential.

Our Purpose

To release restrained potential in all young girls and women.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We behave in an honest, trustworthy and ethical manner.

Team Work

We show respect for the views and contributions of other team members. We listen, support and care for others.

Solidarity

We offer hope and support to all who need assistance.

Professionalism

We value the expertise and skills of workmanship.

Resilience

We instil resilience amongst all women.
